Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Overview

Gobli is deliberately built to collect as little about you as possible. This site is a static page with no account system, no analytics, and no tracking pixels. The Software itself is self-hosted, it runs on your own VPS, not ours, so we never see your activity, your wallet, or your trades.

2. Information We Don't Collect

We don't require an account or email to buy or use Gobli. We don't run server-side analytics, advertising pixels, or session trackers on this website. We never ask for, and never want, your seed phrase or private key.

3. Information You Voluntarily Share

To buy the Sniper, you message us directly on Telegram. Whatever you choose to share in that conversation, your Telegram handle, payment details, and so on, is the only information that passes through to us, and only because you chose to send it. We use it solely to arrange payment and deliver your setup guide and software.

4. Payments

Payment is made directly on-chain in crypto, arranged with us over Telegram. We never process card numbers or bank details, because we never ask for them. Keep in mind that transactions on public blockchains are themselves public and permanent, that's a property of the blockchain, not something this site adds.

5. Third-Party Services

The only external network request this website makes is to Google Fonts, to load the typefaces used on the page. That request is governed by Google's own privacy practices, not ours, and exposes your IP address to Google the same way any font request would. Every other script, including the 3D hero model, the QR code generator, and the donate widget, is vendored locally and never calls out to a CDN or third-party server. Telegram, which we link to and message you through, is an independent third-party platform governed by its own privacy policy, not this one.

6. Cookies

This site does not set cookies and does not use local storage to track you across visits.

7. Data Retention

We don't retain anything beyond the Telegram message you voluntarily send us to claim your download. We don't build a database of customers, wallets, or trading activity, because the self-hosted design means there's nothing here to build it from.

8. Your Rights

If you've messaged us on Telegram and want that conversation deleted, ask and we'll remove it on our end. Since we don't hold an account or profile on you beyond that conversation, there's nothing further to export or erase.

9. Children's Privacy

Gobli is not directed at, and must not be used by, anyone under 18. Cryptocurrency trading carries real financial risk, and we don't knowingly collect information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

11. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent through our Telegram, linked in the footer below.
