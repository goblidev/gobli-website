About Gobli

A synthetic goblin, a self-hosted sniper, no middleman.

What Gobli Is

Gobli is a self-hosted sniper for Solana token launches. Paste a contract in Telegram, and Gobli fires through Jupiter in under 500ms, no chart-staring, no fat-fingering the buy while the candle runs without you.

Why Self-Hosted

Gobli runs on your own VPS, with a wallet you create and control. There's no custody, no middleman, and no team holding your funds at any point. You sign, you send, you sell.

The Character

Gobli is also 100% AI-generated, the art and the attitude are all synthetic. No real person is depicted or impersonated. Think of Gobli as a living mascot for the project: consistent, a little unhinged, and always in the trenches with you.
